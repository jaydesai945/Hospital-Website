Orthopedics

Orthopedics, as a medical specialty, focuses on the diagnosis, treatment, and management of conditions and injuries affecting the musculoskeletal system. Orthopedic specialists, also known as orthopedic surgeons or orthopedists, undergo extensive training to become experts in this field.

Fractures and Trauma

Orthopedic surgeons treat fractures (broken bones) and traumatic injuries to the musculoskeletal system, such as dislocations and ligament tears.

Sports Medicine

Orthopedic specialists in sports medicine focus on the treatment and prevention of sports-related injuries, including ligament tears (such as ACL tears), tendon injuries, and stress fractures.

Arthritis Management

Orthopedic surgeons manage arthritis, including osteoarthritis and rheumatoid arthritis, through various treatment options such as medications, injections, physical therapy, and surgery when necessary.

Spine Disorders

Orthopedic spine surgeons treat conditions affecting the spine, including herniated discs, spinal stenosis, degenerative disc disease, scoliosis, and spinal deformities.

Pediatric Orthopedics

Pediatric orthopedic surgeons specialize in the diagnosis and treatment of musculoskeletal conditions and injuries in children, including congenital disorders, developmental abnormalities, and pediatric fractures.

Hand and Upper Extremity Surgery

surgeons specializing in hand and upper extremity surgery treat conditions affecting the hand, wrist, forearm, and elbow, such as carpal tunnel syndrome, tendon injuries, and fractures.

Orthopedic Oncology

Orthopedic oncologists specialize in the diagnosis and treatment of bone and soft tissue tumors, including both benign and malignant tumors.

Foot and Ankle Surgery

Orthopedic surgeons specializing in foot and ankle surgery diagnose and treat conditions affecting the foot and ankle, including fractures, tendon injuries, bunions, and ankle arthritis..
